\section*{Executing on Real Quantum Computer}

The code was executed on ibm\_brisbane due to immediate availability, but the results were wrong Fig. \ref{brisbane_counts} . FakeSherbrooke was more reliable during experiments Fig. \ref{fakeshercounts}, and I executed my circuit on a ibm\_sherbrooke backend, but with noticeable noise. During all Assignments, I've noticed that Brisbane is not responding reliably in more complex circuits, while Sherbrooke provides less noisy results. Exporting Operations metrics, I can observe that Brisbane has fewer operations after transpilation, which may explain the more noisy results.
